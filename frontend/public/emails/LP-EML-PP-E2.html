LAUNCHPATH  ·  OPERATING STANDARD

How the 90-Day Survival System builds on the Four Pillars.

 

The LaunchPath Standard is built on four compliance pillars. Each pillar protects your operating authority from a different failure vector. The 90-day installation sequence is designed to build each pillar in order — not randomly, and not on a timeline you define.

The sequence is not arbitrary. Authority Protection and Insurance Continuity are installed first because they are the fastest failure vectors. A carrier can lose operating authority or insurance continuity before a single compliance audit ever occurs.

— LP-SYS-01 — Authority Protection: Filing continuity, FMCSA compliance, audit readiness
— LP-SYS-02 — Insurance Continuity: BMC-91 filings, coverage gaps, policy alignment
— LP-SYS-03 — Compliance Backbone: HOS, D&A program, driver qualification files, vehicle maintenance
— LP-SYS-04 — Cash-Flow Oxygen: Rate discipline, cost-per-mile, financial runway management
