Before kickoff — complete these three items.

 

Kickoff is coming. Before that session, there are three things you should complete. They are not optional — they are pre-conditions for the kickoff session to be productive.

These three items represent the starting point for your Compliance Backbone installation. If you do not know where you are on these three before kickoff, the first session will be spent on diagnosis instead of installation.

— Review your current driver file — identify what is present and what is missing
— Confirm your D&A program enrollment status — enrolled, not enrolled, or unknown
— Locate your most recent vehicle inspection records — gather whatever exists now
