LAUNCHPATH  ·  OPERATING STANDARD

Why LaunchPath isn't for everyone.

 

LaunchPath is a compliance operating system. It is designed to install on top of an active, functioning carrier operation. It is not a startup guide. It is not a how-to-get-your-authority program. It is not designed for operators who are still in the pre-authority phase.

Your NO-GO result indicated that one or more foundational requirements are not yet in place. Attempting to install the Standard without that foundation produces incomplete results at best — and a false sense of compliance coverage at worst.

There is no penalty for where you are. The carriers who eventually complete the Standard successfully are often the ones who were honest about their starting point.

 

A system installed on an unstable foundation will fail before the audit arrives.
